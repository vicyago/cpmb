CPMB - Center for Plant Molecular Breeding

We are a research network which focus on the selection and development of tropical forage grasses and sugarcane cultivars based on modern molecular tools, statistical methods and efficient computational resources.

The CPMB is an interdisciplinary team organized in four JRAs (Joint Research Actions)


Interested in: Plant breeding, Plant Genetics, Bioinformatics, Statistical Genetics, Phytopathology, Computational Biology, Phenomics, Genomics, Plant Biotechnology, Artificial Intelligence.
